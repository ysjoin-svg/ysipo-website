Our Story

Committed to IP Excellence

Yong Syu Intellectual Property Office operates under the core principle of "Sustainable Service, Quality First." For over two decades, we have been dedicated to providing world-class intellectual property services to clients around the globe — from early-stage startups to established multinational corporations.

With our headquarters in Zuoying, Kaohsiung and a branch office in Zhongzheng, Taipei, our dual-office structure allows us to serve businesses and individuals throughout Taiwan with convenient, hands-on assistance.

Our service scope spans 50+ countries and regions worldwide, covering the three major IP domains of patents, trademarks, and copyright, helping clients build a solid protection framework in international markets.
