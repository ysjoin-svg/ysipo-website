Patent Knowledge

Why Prior Art Search is Essential Before Filing a Patent

Many inventors rush to file without conducting a prior art search. Confirming your invention's novelty beforehand not only saves significant filing costs, but also helps draft claims with broader and stronger protection scope.

Trademark Knowledge

First-to-File Principle: Why You Must Register Your Trademark Early

Taiwan and most countries follow the "first-to-file" principle. Brand recognition alone does not grant trademark rights. If a third party registers your brand name first, you may be forced to rebrand or pay a high price to reclaim it.

Copyright Knowledge

Copyright Exists Automatically — So Why Should You Register It?

While copyright protection arises automatically upon creation, formal registration establishes official legal evidence of the creation date. In infringement disputes, a registration certificate becomes a powerful weapon to defend your rights.
