Patent Services

Trademark Services

Trademark Services

YSIPO has been recognized multiple times as one of Taiwan's Top-20 trademark applicant firms — a testament to over 25 years of deep trademark practice. Our client base spans local brands, multinationals, and startups, and our filings extend to over 50 countries through the Madrid System and direct national filings.

We are particularly recognized for our trademark dispute and rescue capabilities. When a mark faces refusal, opposition, or invalidation, our team brings rigorous legal argumentation, substantial evidence strategies, and deep familiarity with TIPO's examination standards to maximize the chance of a successful outcome.

Trademark Rescue

An office action is not the end. YSIPO guides clients from initial refusal through response, appeal, and administrative litigation — with a track record of successfully reversing difficult decisions.
