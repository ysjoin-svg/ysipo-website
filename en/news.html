TIPO Announces Expanded Electronic Examination of Trademark Figurative Elements

Taiwan's Intellectual Property Office (TIPO) has announced an expansion of its electronic examination procedures for trademark figurative elements, alongside an upgrade to its image-search comparison system. These measures are expected to improve examination efficiency and reduce initial review wait times. Applicants are advised to verify that their logo files meet the updated specification and resolution standards before submission to avoid processing delays.

One Year of the UPC: What Taiwan Companies Should Know About European Patent Strategy

The Unified Patent Court (UPC) and Unitary Patent system have now been operating for over one year. The system allows applicants to obtain patent protection across 17 EU member states through a single filing, significantly reducing the administrative cost of European IP portfolios. Taiwan companies with European market ambitions should reassess whether to convert existing European validated patents to Unitary Patent format, and how to structure future European filings under the UPC framework. Contact YSIPO for guidance.
