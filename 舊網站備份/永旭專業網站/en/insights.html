FAQ

How long does a patent take to grant in Taiwan?

Taiwan invention patents average 2–3 years for substantive examination. Utility model patents (formal review only) take around 6–10 months. Design patents average 12–18 months. PPH (Patent Prosecution Highway) can accelerate invention patents to approximately 1 year.

Is a trademark refusal the end of the road?

Not at all. After a refusal, you still have response, re-examination, administrative appeal, and even administrative litigation available. YSIPO has a strong track record of reversing refusals through strategic arguments — many marks our clients were told were "impossible" were ultimately registered.

Do I need to register my copyright in Taiwan?

Under Taiwan's Copyright Act, copyright protection is automatic upon creation — no registration required. However, copyright registration serves as valuable evidence in disputes. For commercially important works, registration is still advisable.
